Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Summary: Our extensions process everything locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data. Period.

Scope

This privacy policy applies to all browser extensions published by SandwichDev ("we", "us", "our") on the Chrome Web Store, Firefox Add-ons, and Microsoft Edge Add-ons, including but not limited to:

  • WCAG Accessibility Auditor
  • Privacy & Cookie Compliance Scanner
  • Contract Clause Highlighter
  • Client Time Tracker Pro
  • Invoice Data Extractor
  • Gmail Big Unsubscribe
  • GitHub Line Numbers Always
  • Old Reddit Redirect
  • And all other extensions published under the SandwichDev developer account

Data Collection

Our extensions do not collect any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Authentication tokens or credentials
  • IP addresses or geolocation data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Financial or payment information

Local Processing Only

All of our extensions perform 100% of their processing locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers, APIs, or third-party services. All analysis, scanning, highlighting, tracking, and extraction happens entirely within your browser.

Data Storage

Some extensions use chrome.storage.local (or the equivalent browser storage API) to save user preferences and settings locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is automatically deleted when the extension is uninstalled
  • Can be cleared at any time through your browser's extension settings

Permissions

Our extensions request only the minimum browser permissions necessary for their functionality:

  • activeTab — To access the current page content when you explicitly activate the extension
  • storage — To save your preferences locally on your device
  • tabs — To identify the current page URL (used for features like URL-based time tracking)
  • idle — Used only by Client Time Tracker Pro to detect when you're away from the computer

No extension requests access to your browsing history, bookmarks, downloads, or any data beyond what is needed for its stated functionality.

Third-Party Services

Our extensions do not integrate with, connect to, or transmit data to any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs.

Children's Privacy

Our extensions are general-purpose tools and do not knowingly target or collect information from children under the age of 13.

Premium/Paid Features

Some extensions offer premium features via Stripe subscription. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe. We do not handle, store, or have access to your payment card information. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our extensions after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, export, or delete. If you have concerns about any data stored locally by an extension, you can:

  • Clear extension data through your browser settings
  • Uninstall the extension, which removes all locally stored data
