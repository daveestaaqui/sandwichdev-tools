Privacy Policy — WCAG Accessibility Auditor

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Summary: WCAG Accessibility Auditor processes everything locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

About This Extension

scanning web pages for WCAG 2.1 AA compliance including color contrast, alt text, ARIA roles, heading hierarchy, form labels, and keyboard navigation

Data Collection

WCAG Accessibility Auditor does not collect any personal data. We do not collect names, email addresses, browsing history, IP addresses, usage analytics, or any other personally identifiable information.

Local Processing

All functionality — including scanning web pages for WCAG 2.1 AA compliance including color contrast, alt text, ARIA roles, heading hierarchy, form labels, and keyboard navigation — runs 100% locally in your browser. No data is sent to external servers, APIs, or third-party services.

Data Storage

This extension uses chrome.storage.local to save user preferences on your device. This data never leaves your device, is not accessible to us, and is deleted when you uninstall the extension.

Permissions Used

  • activeTab — Access the current page when you click the extension
  • storage — Save your audit preferences locally

No permissions beyond those listed above are requested.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not connect to any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs.

Premium Features

Premium features are available via Stripe subscription. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store your payment information. See Stripe's privacy policy.

Changes

Updates to this policy will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
